Meatball Recipe

Description

The best Meatballs on the Planet. Made by Grandpa.

  • Ground meat.
  • Seasoning and herbs.
  • Eggs.
  • Cheese.
  • Italian Bread.
  • Water.
  • Olive oil.
  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Add the bread crums and slowly add water.
  3. Shape the mixture into balls.
  4. Fry the meatballs until they're all brown and crisp.
